India is the world's largest democracy with a rich cultural heritage spanning thousands of years.
The country is known for its incredible diversity, with 22 official languages and numerous traditions.
Indian cuisine is famous worldwide for its unique blend of spices and flavors.
With a population of over 1.4 billion people, India is the world's most populous country.
The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, is one of the Seven Wonders of the World and a symbol of India's architectural brilliance.
India is also known for its contributions to science, mathematics, and technology throughout history.
